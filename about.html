Summary

Chengming Li (ææˆæ˜), Ph.D., associate professor, Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology(SIAT), Chinese Academy of Science(CAS).

I'm now an associate professor at Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology(SIAT), Chinese Academy of Science(CAS), China. I obtained Shenzhen Overseas High-level Talents Award in 2015. I received the Ph.D. degree in Graduate School of Information Science and Electrical Engineering from Kyushu University, Japan, in 2015. As the principle investigator, I participated in multiple research projects including 1 Nature Science Foundation of China project, 2 Shenzhen Fundamental Research Foundation projects, and 1 Shenzhen Key Technologies project. My research interests include Information-Centric Networking, Software Defined Networking, Internet of Things, and Mobile Edge Computing. I have published over 20 high quality journal and conference papers.
