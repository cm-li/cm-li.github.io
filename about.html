个人简介

李成明 (Chengming Li)，博士，中国科学院深圳先进技术研究院，数字所，副研究员。

深圳市海外高层次人才、深圳市南山区领航人才入选者。分别于2009年7月和2011年12月在大连理工大学软件学院获得工学学士和工学硕士学位；于2015年3月在日本九州大学系统情报学府获得工学博士学位。 作为项目负责人，主持多项科研课题，包括国家自然科学基金1项、深圳市基础研究2项。主要研究兴趣包括未来智能网络，信息中心网络、物联网、深度强化学习、数据挖掘、网络安全等。

Summary

Chengming Li (李成明), Ph.D., associate professor, Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology (SIAT), Chinese Academy of Science (CAS).

I'm now an associate professor at Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology (SIAT), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), China. I obtained Shenzhen Overseas High-level Talents Award in 2015. I received the Ph.D. degree in Graduate School of Information Science and Electrical Engineering from Kyushu University, Japan, in 2015. As the principle investigator, I participated in multiple research projects including 1 National Natural Science Foundation of China project, 2 Shenzhen Fundamental Research Foundation projects, and 1 Shenzhen Key Technologies project. My research interests include Information-Centric Networking, Mobile Edge Computing, Internet of Things, Deep Reinforcement Learning, Data Mining, and Network Security.
